#+title: internet of things
#+filetags: :computer_science:
- invented by Kevin Ashton.
- the word /thing/ was taken from Neil Gershenfeld work "*Things that Think*"
- was first introduced to the community in 1999 for supply chain management
- Definion: "making a *things* sense its enviroments and collect information 
  without the aid of human intervention"
