#+title: Aldous Huxley   

Aldous Leonard Huxley (26 July 1894 – 22 November 1963)
an English writer and philosopher.
He wrote nearly 50 books —both novels and non-fiction works—
as well as wide-ranging essays, narratives, and poems.
