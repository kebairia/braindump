#+title: zettelkasten

- their are two papers written by [[id:e2faf5e6-e2b4-4328-a101-5b4a8f56da8e][niklas luhman]], and they are translated and uploaded on that [[https://luhmann.surge.sh/][site]]
- their is a similar but an old approche for storing ideas, and a way of a fast access for these ideas with the [[id:774c601f-cf35-45f1-829d-4ad2912aadc2][memex]] project.
