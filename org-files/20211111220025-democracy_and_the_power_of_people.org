#+title: democracy and the power of people
Many people use the idea of [[id:0b730bba-3af4-4b21-8379-475f504af711][democracy]] as an emotive tool to manipulate people into believing
that they can rule themselves, but in reality, the worth of one vote -- which is the only thing a voter can do --
represents nothing and has no effect on how policies and rules can change in a country.
