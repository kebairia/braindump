#+title: Gustave Le Bon
Charles-Marie Gustave Le Bon was a leading French [[id:49136353-90b1-4a88-a8fe-9b0c1f31946b][polymath]] whose areas of interest included anthropology, psychology, sociology, medicine, invention, and physics. He is best known for his 1895 work [[id:c6b51578-b384-4a69-a371-badfd7a60f4f][The Crowd]]: A Study of the Popular Mind, which is considered one of the seminal works of crowd
