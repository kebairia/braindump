#+title: ant colony
for an *Ant colony* to succeed, it has to consist of a group of members with a specific and _simple_ design.
so that every member can do one job, and one job only.
any complexity (in the design of members --ants--) can leads for some sort of [[id:fd5a4c5f-b137-4ed4-bb01-b49178026185][independence]] which may undermine the colony as a whole.
