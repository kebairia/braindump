#+title: Industrial Society and Its Future

*Industrial Society and Its Future*, also known by the *Unabomber Manifesto*, a 35,000 word essay by [[id:7128efe6-5518-4fd3-87c5-2cb5d208dc9c][Ted Kaczynski]] contending that the [[id:8ff6d723-ea79-43ba-81c3-8d59989bb51b][industrial revolution]] began a harmful process of technology destroying nature, while forcing humans to adapt to machines, and creating a sociopolitical order that suppresses human freedom and potential.
