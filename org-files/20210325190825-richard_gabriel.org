#+title: Richard Gabriel
#+roam_tags: unix "computer science"
an American computer scientist (born 1949) known for his work in computing related to the programming language [[file:20210325191049-lisp.org][lisp]], and especially [[file:20210325191110-common_lisp.org][common lisp]].
His best known work was a 1990 essay "Lisp: Good News, Bad News, How to Win Big", which introduced the phrase [[file:20210325190256-worse_is_better.org][worse is better]]
