#+title: zettelkasten
#+filetags: :memory:notes:

- zettelkasten is a note taking method that tries to mimic the brain neurons structure, invented by [[id:e2faf5e6-e2b4-4328-a101-5b4a8f56da8e][niklas luhman]]
- their are two papers written by [[id:e2faf5e6-e2b4-4328-a101-5b4a8f56da8e][niklas luhman]], and they are translated and uploaded on that [[https://luhmann.surge.sh/][site]]
- their is a similar but an old approche for storing ideas, and a way of a fast access for these ideas with the [[id:774c601f-cf35-45f1-829d-4ad2912aadc2][memex]] project.
- This is a great [[https://niklas-luhmann-archiv.de/][website]] niklas luhman's work, his notes archive, and many other things. 

- every note here (an index card) called a [[id:379a19e2-4229-481e-9355-b83b2e5239d4][zettel]]
